Operational Flow

A Floor That Runs Itself End-to-End.

Four operational layers, one infrastructure surface. Every layer is managed, monitored, and continuously optimized by the engineering team.

1
Provision

Hire an agent — the engine clones a CRM, allocates credentials, and onboards the seat into your workspace.

2
Route

Leads are injected from the master vault into active stations on demand — single, batched, or floor-wide.

3
Operate

Telemetry, leaderboards, and floor-wide broadcasts run continuously from the central command surface.

4
Recycle

Inactive seats are vaulted, leads recycled back to inventory, the floor stays clean and audit-ready.
