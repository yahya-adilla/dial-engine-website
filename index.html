⚙️Agent Lifecycle & Station Control

Instant Agent Provisioning

New hires are onboarded in seconds. The system automatically provisions a fully configured CRM workspace, assigns active telephony credentials, and delivers login details directly to the agent — zero manual setup required from management.

One-Command Termination

A single command revokes all workspace access, wipes the assigned CRM, reclaims telephony credentials, and vaults the station back into the ready pool — cleanly and instantly. The floor never stops moving.

Station Recovery & Reuse

No station is ever wasted. Terminated and recycled workspaces are automatically re-pooled and made available for the next hire, eliminating infrastructure bloat and ensuring a permanent supply of deployable seats at zero marginal cost.

🗄️Lead Vault & Distribution Engine

Precision Vault Injection

Route any quantity of leads to any agent with a single command. Inject directly into a single station, execute a calibrated distribution across a subset of agents, or trigger a full floor-wide mass injection in seconds.

Shuffle & Redistribution

Combat list fatigue before it kills momentum. Wipe active CRMs, randomize the master vault, and re-deal fresh batches across the floor — keeping dialers on new contacts and conversion rates from decaying mid-shift.

Inventory Recovery

Every lead is accounted for. Partial or full batches assigned to inactive, recycled, or terminated agents are instantly reclaimed and returned to the live vault — ensuring no inventory is ever stranded or lost.

📡Real-Time Telemetry & KPI Operations

Live Floor Dashboard

Pull a live snapshot of the entire floor at any moment — agent status, current CRM inventory per seat, vault depth, telephony line availability, and active session counts — all in a single command from the operational hub.

Shift-Persistent KPI Tracking

Daily dials and conversion metrics are tracked continuously across the full shift, surviving CRM wipes, mid-shift recycling, and new lead injections. Managers always see the true running total — not a reset counter.

Automated Leaderboards & Broadcasts

Gamify the floor with live-updated daily and all-time leaderboards. Push private KPI briefings directly to management, or broadcast floor-wide updates and alerts to all active agents simultaneously from the command hub.

📞Telephony Integration & Dialing Infrastructure

Frictionless One-Click Dialing

Agents dial without ever copying a number. The system dynamically renders all contacts as live clickable links within the CRM — a single click bridges directly to the telephony client and places the call instantly, eliminating manual entry entirely.

Automated SIP Credential Routing

Each agent seat is assigned a dedicated telephony line with a unique extension and authentication profile. Credentials are automatically generated, stored, and dispatched during onboarding — and can be re-issued to any agent on demand in seconds.

Enterprise Routing Layer (Add-On)

For floors requiring warm transfers, managed inbound queues, or internal extension networks, ZLF Systems deploys a fully configured Private Branch Exchange layer — enabling advanced call routing and transfer architecture natively within the operational stack.
